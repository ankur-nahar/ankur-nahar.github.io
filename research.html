Research

Research Assistant at Wills Eye Hospital

I have been privileged to work alongside numerous mentors including Dr. Julia Haller, Dr. Sunir Garg, Dr. Ajay Kuriyan, Dr. Yoshihiro Yonekawa, and many others at MidAtlantic Retina. Here I led teams of medical students and research fellows to parse and analyze large volumes of data. I also utilized Python and other online services to accelerate and automate data collection and analysis. My current projects include:

  • Writing a manuscript examining gender composition of editorial boards across all major specialties in medicine

  • Completed manuscript analyzing the incidence and outcomes of delayed-onset retinal re-detachment

  • Completed data collection for and analysis of the use of anti-VEGF injections for retinal vein occlusions with good baseline vision

  • Assisted in creating an online survey to assess follow-up compliance in patients with diabetic retinopathy sent to over 1,000 patients

Research Assistant at Ophthalmic Consultants of Boston

Through the mentorship of Dr. Rebecca Soares, I have expanded on one of our most successful projects (“Who is an ‘Eye Doctor” published in JAMA Ophthalmology) gathering metrics assessing search engine optimization of all ophthalmic and optometry practice websites in the US with the goal of understanding what enables improved visibility on Google. I have also completed data collection for a project assessing disparities in vision care for Native Americans.

Research Assistant at Cho Lab

With Dr. Seo-Hee Cho at Temple University and later Sidney Kimmel Medical College, I helped develop a mouse model for rare eye disease Leber congenital amaurosis type 8 (LCA8). During my time in the lab, I designed a visual cliff assay and conducted ocular coherence tomography, optokinetic, and electrophysiological assays to investigate markers of retinal degeneration in mice. I conducted maxiprep of plasmid DNA, retinal electroporation, and site-directed mutagenesis to explore potential gene therapy solutions. I later initiated an idea for and published a review paper comparing existing models for LCA8 in Developmental Dynamics.

Shields Ocular Oncology Summer Program

I rotated with Dr. Carol Shields for 6 weeks coordinating clinic and OR flow. Alongside a team of medical students, we developed one of the largest-ever databases examining longitudinal outcomes of uveal melanoma with over 8,000 patients spanning 50 years. My work has resulted in a manuscript currently undergoing revisions titled "Uveal Melanoma: Outcomes of A Rare but Deadly Eye Cancer in Children and Teenagers in 114 Cases" I also published a case report titled, "Cystoid macular edema secondary to extra-macular choroidal melanoma."

Video Submission for Video Journal of Cataract, Refractive, and Glaucoma Surgery (VJCRGS)

I storyboarded, edited, and contributed to a video with Dr. Geoffrey Tabin at Byers Eye Institute as part of a collaboration explaining sutureless extracapsular cataract surgery, commissioned by the VJCRGS.

Section Editor for American Journal of Medical Quality

I provided comprehensive evaluations and constructive feedback for each article in the Quality Training to Improve Performance (QTIP) section of the American Journal of Medical Quality.

Speaker at 2nd Annual Scholarly Inquiry Student Project Showcase

I was one of five students, selected from a competitive pool of applicants, to speak to the first-year class at Sidney Kimmel Medical College. My presentation centered on how students can integrate creativity into research.

Writer for InSIGHT Journal

I wrote and published an article discussing manual small incision cataract surgery and the origins of the Himalayan Cataract Project with Dr. Brenton Finklea at Wills Eye.
