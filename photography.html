Photography

Landscape and nature photography has long been a passion of mine. Capturing the destination of an arduous trek is as much a representation of achievement as it is an artistic expression. My journeys leave me with a sense of sublime awe that I aspire to capture in these photographs. This selection was taken with a Canon T3i and a kit lens.
